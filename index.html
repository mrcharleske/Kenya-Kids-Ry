Our Mission

At Kenya Kids Ry, we believe every child deserves a chance. Since 2012, we have been committed to transforming the lives of children from the slums by providing education, safe homes, and mentorship. Our vision is to see every child rise above poverty and become a responsible, independent citizen of Kenya.
