Welcome to Kenya Kids Ry

Founded in 2012 by Katja Maria, we bring hope, education, and brighter futures for children in Kenya.

Our Mission

At Kenya Kids Ry, we believe that every child deserves an equal chance to grow, learn, and dream. Through education, care, and community support, we empower children to overcome challenges and step into a brighter tomorrow.
