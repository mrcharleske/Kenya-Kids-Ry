About Us

Meet Katja Maria

Katja Maria, a humanitarian with a heart for children, founded Kenya Kids Ry out of a deep passion to transform lives through love, care, and education. Her journey began with a single step of faith, reaching out to children living in hardship and providing them with hope.

Through her leadership and dedication, Katja has been instrumental in building bridges of opportunity for children who otherwise would be left behind. She strongly believes that every child deserves the chance to discover their potential and live a life of dignity.

About Kenya Kids Ry

Kenya Kids Ry is a child-focused initiative dedicated to providing education, healthcare, and mentorship to vulnerable children across Kenya. Our mission is to break the cycle of poverty by empowering children with tools to succeed.

We focus not only on academics, but also on holistic growth — ensuring children receive the love, care, and emotional support they need to thrive. Over the years, Kenya Kids Ry has impacted hundreds of lives, proving that with compassion and community, transformation is possible.
