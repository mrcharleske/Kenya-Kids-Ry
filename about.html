About Kenya Kids Ry

Meet Katja Maria

Kenya Kids Ry was founded in 2012 by Katja Maria, driven by a passion to transform the lives of children in Kenya. Katja was deeply moved by the struggles of children living in slums and chose to provide them with education, safe homes, and mentorship for a brighter future.

Our Journey

Since 2012, Kenya Kids Ry has supported children in moving from life in slums to safer environments and better opportunities. Many have completed their basic education, while others are already working and living as responsible citizens of Kenya. Our mission remains simple: to give every child a chance to succeed.
