About Kenya Kids Ry

Founded in 2012 by Katja Maria, Kenya Kids Ry is dedicated to transforming the lives of vulnerable children through education, healthcare, and community support.

Meet Katja Maria

Katja Maria, the visionary behind Kenya Kids Ry, believed that every child deserves love, dignity, and opportunity. Since 2012, her mission has been to empower children and build stronger communities.

Our Work

Kenya Kids Ry partners with local communities to provide education, healthcare, and rehabilitation services. Together, we create hope, inspire change, and build a brighter future for children.
