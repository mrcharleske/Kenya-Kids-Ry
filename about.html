About Kenya Kids Ry

Founded in 2012 by Katja Maria, Kenya Kids Ry is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of children in Kenya. We believe every child deserves care, education, and the chance for a brighter future.

Our Founder

Katja Maria began this journey with compassion and determination. Her vision was to create an organization that brings hope, opportunity, and dignity to children who need it most.

Our Mission

Kenya Kids Ry works through local partnerships and community engagement to deliver healthcare, education, and rehabilitation services for children, making lasting impacts.
